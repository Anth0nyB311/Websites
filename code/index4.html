Features of the app:

Live Chat


- Our app will offer a free service where people can have quick access to doctors that we hire.

- Our doctors could give you advice on treatments for your condition or even give possible diagnostics of your condition, they will also judge whether to refer you to a local private doctor or specialists if needed. 

- This service provides a fast track to conventional free GP visits for mild health issues. For conditions requiring specialists, our doctors will direct you to a reliable private health centre.
